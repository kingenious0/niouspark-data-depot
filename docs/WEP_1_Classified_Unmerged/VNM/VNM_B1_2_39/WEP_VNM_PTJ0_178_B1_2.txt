"I partly agree with the idea that college students should have a part-time job.

On the one hand, a part-time job helps students earn extra money. Many students need to pay for daily expenses, books, and sometimes tuition. With a job, they can be more independent and support their families. In addition, students can get real experience from work. They can improve soft skills like communication, teamwork, and time management. These are very useful for their future careers. On the other hand, working part-time takes time and energy. If students work too much, they may feel tired and have less time to study. This can lead to lower grades and stress. Some students cannot balance between studying and working, which can affect their academic life. In my opinion, college students can have a part-time job, but they need to know how to manage their time well. If they have good planning, they can both study and work successfully.
